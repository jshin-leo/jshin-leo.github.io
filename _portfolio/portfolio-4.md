### HR System Evaluation and Modernization Initiative

**Organization:** Hyundai Engineering & Construction  
**Role:** HR Task Force Team Member  

**Overview**   
Contributed to an enterprise-wide initiative to evaluate and modernize HR information systems, with a focus on data architecture, operational efficiency, and long-term scalability.  

**Key Contributions**  
- Served as a core member of the HR Task Force responsible for assessing existing HR systems and underlying data structures.  
- Analyzed workflow inefficiencies and system limitations to inform selection of scalable, enterprise-wide HR solutions.  
- Supported cross-functional discussions to align technical recommendations with organizational needs and long-term modernization goals.
