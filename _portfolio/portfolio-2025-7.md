### Graduate Coursework Projects – 2025

**Program:** M.S. in Computer Science  
**Institution:** University of Texas at El Paso  
**Project Period:** Spring & Fall 2025  

**Overview**  
Selected project-based coursework completed as part of the M.S. program.

---

### Spring 2025

**Integrated Problem Solving for AI** *(Group Project)*  
Developed machine learning models to predict chronic diseases associated with Adverse Childhood Experiences (ACEs) using CDC BRFSS 2023 data, with an emphasis on problem formulation, data ethics, and real-world applicability.

**Data Mining** *(Group Project)*  
Studied and presented resilient k-center clustering, analyzing stability guarantees and robustness under noisy data using graph-based methods and theoretical performance bounds.

---

### Fall 2025

**Deep Learning** *(Individual Project)*  
Developed a deep learning–based intake classification system for counseling settings, using MentalChat16K and LLM-assisted labeling to support topic categorization of client intake transcripts.

**Master’s Thesis** *(Individual Project)*  
Conducted independent research on meta-learning for adaptive and generalizable cybersecurity systems, focusing on few-shot malware detection and meta-reinforcement learning in dynamic network environments.
