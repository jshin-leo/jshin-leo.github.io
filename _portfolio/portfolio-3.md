### Legal Affairs Systems Upgrade

**Organization:** Hyundai AutoEver  
**Role:** Project Manager  
**Project Period:** June 2019 – October 2019   

**Overview**  
Led a enterprise systems upgrade to modernize legal operations related to financial deposits and court-managed processes.  

**Key Contributions**
- Managed the upgrade of internal systems supporting deposit, provisional attachment, and court deposit (consignation) workflows.  
- Coordinated requirements and implementation efforts to improve accuracy, compliance, and operational efficiency in legal affairs processes.  
- Oversaw project timelines and stakeholder communication to ensure successful delivery within a condensed project window.
