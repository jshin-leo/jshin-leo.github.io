### Understanding Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning: Foundational Concepts for Counselor Education  

**Organization:** Journal of Counselor Preparation and Supervision    
**Role:** Co-Author    
**Project Period:** August-September 2025  
**Publication:** Han, E. & Shin, J. (2025). Understanding Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning: Foundational Concepts for Counselor Education. Journal of Counselor Preparation and Supervision, 19(3), 1-15. http://dx.doi.org/10.70013/7b5l47yn

**Overview**  
Co-authored a peer-reviewed publication introducing foundational concepts of artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) for counselor educators, supervisors, and counseling students. The article translates technical AI/ML concepts into accessible language and demonstrates how emerging technologies may influence counselor education, training, and research.

**Key Contributions**  
- Co-developed an interdisciplinary framework explaining core AI and machine learning concepts for counselor education audiences.  
- Provided applied examples demonstrating how AI/ML approaches can support counseling research and training.  
- Presented the work as an **Education Session at the Association for Assessment and Research in Counseling (AARC)** 2025 conference.  
- Recognized by the **Association for Counselor Education and Supervision (ACES)** as one of the **most accessed articles of 2025**, featured in the **ACES Newsletter (December 23, 2025)**.
