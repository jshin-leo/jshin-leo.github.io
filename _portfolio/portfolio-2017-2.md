### Year-End Tax Reporting System Upgrade  

**Organization:** Hyundai AutoEver  
**Role:** System Administrator & Developer    
**Project Period:** Dec 2017 – Mar 2018; Dec 2018 – Mar 2019; Dec 2019 – Mar 2020  

**Overview**  
Independently upgraded and maintained the enterprise payroll tax reporting system to comply with annual Korean tax law updates and support large-scale employee tax filings.  

**Key Contributions**
- Upgraded payroll and year-end tax reporting modules to align with annual Korean tax regulations.  
- Processed and validated year-end tax reports for **5,000+ Hyundai Engineering & Construction employees**.  
- Ensured accurate electronic submission of tax records to the **Korean National Tax Service (NTS)**.  
- Maintained system stability during peak reporting periods and coordinated with HR and finance teams to resolve data discrepancies.
