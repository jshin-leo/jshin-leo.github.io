### Enhancing Counselor Education through AI Chatbots: A Mixed-Methods Study  

**Organization:** Counselor Education Research Collaboration  
**Role:** Research Contributor (Qualitative Analysis)    
**Project Period:** September–November 2025    

**Overview**  
Contributed to a mixed-methods research project examining how artificial intelligence (AI) chatbots can support counselor education and training. The study explored how counselor trainees interact with AI-assisted learning tools and evaluated the pedagogical implications of integrating AI technologies into counseling education environments.

**Key Contributions**  
- Conducted qualitative coding of participant responses and organized thematic categories for analysis.  
- Synthesized qualitative findings to support the interpretation of student experiences with AI chatbot tools.  
- Assisted with structuring thematic results for manuscript development and integration with quantitative findings.  

**Publication Status**  
- Manuscript submitted: November 10, 2025
