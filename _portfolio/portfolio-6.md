### Faculty Spin-off Pipeline Analysis & Commercialization Strategy  

**Organization:** Office of Technology Commercialization (OTC), UTEP  
**Role:** Graduate Research Fellow / Analyst  

**Overview**  
Conducted an institutional analysis of UTEP’s faculty commercialization ecosystem to identify barriers to research-based spin-off development and propose strategic improvements.  

**Key Contributions**  
- Led structured interviews with three faculty founders actively pursuing spin-off ventures to identify systemic policy and process constraints.  
- Evaluated gaps in time-allocation policies, internal funding mechanisms, mentorship structures, and commercialization guidance.  
- Authored a strategic report outlining actionable recommendations to strengthen UTEP’s faculty spin-off pipeline, including policy flexibility, seed funding models, mentorship networks, and investor engagement pathways.
