### Grant Research & Funding Strategy – MLCIC  

**Organization:** Mike Loya Center for Innovation and Commerce (MLCIC), UTEP  
**Role:** Graduate Research Fellow  
**Project Period:** Spring 2025  

**Overview**  
Conducted a comprehensive funding landscape analysis to identify grant and sponsorship opportunities supporting entrepreneurship education, student programs, and innovation initiatives at MLCIC.

**Key Contributions**
- Researched federal, state, and private funding opportunities aligned with MLCIC’s mission, including entrepreneurship education, ecosystem development, and workforce innovation.  
- Identified and evaluated **six targeted funding opportunities**, detailing eligibility criteria, funding ranges, timelines, and strategic fit.  
- Analyzed peer entrepreneurship centers at comparable institutions to benchmark funding models and successful grant strategies.  
- Produced actionable recommendations prioritizing grants MLCIC should pursue to support internships, programs, speakers, and long-term initiative sustainability.
