**Project**: Faculty Spin-off Pipeline Analysis & Commercialization Strategy    
• **Organization**: Office of Technology Commercialization (OTC), UTEP    
• **Role**: Graduate Research Fellow / Analyst 
• Conducted structured interviews with three faculty founders to identify institutional barriers to research-based spin-off creation.  
• Analyzed policy, funding, mentorship, and process gaps affecting commercialization readiness.  
• Wrote a strategic report with actionable recommendations to strengthen UTEP’s faculty spin-off pipeline, including policy flexibility, seed funding, mentorship networks, and investor engagement pathways.
