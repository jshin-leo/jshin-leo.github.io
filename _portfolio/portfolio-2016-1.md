### Legal Affairs Systems Integration

**Organization:** Hyundai AutoEver  
**Role:** Project Manager  
**Project Period:** June 2016 – December 2016  

**Overview**  
Led a multi-entity systems integration project to support legal operations across affiliated companies.  

**Key Contributions**
- Upgraded and integrated legal affairs systems to support operations for both Hyundai Engineering & Construction and Hyundai Engineering.  
- Standardized legal workflows and data structures across organizations to improve consistency and operational efficiency.  
- Coordinated cross-company stakeholders to ensure seamless system integration and uninterrupted legal operations.
